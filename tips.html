Foods:

  • Eat local food
  • Limit meat consumption
  • Use reusable bags for shopping
  • Buy only what is needed to reduce food waste.



    • Clothing:

  • Take good care of your clothes
  • Try wearing second-hand clothes
  • Use clothes that are made from recycled material



    • Energy and Transportation:

  • Use public transportation, if possible cycle
  • If possible try lowering the heat at your home by 1 degree
  • Do not waste water
  • Do not waste electricity, unplug your device from charge if your battery is already full
  • Limit and recycle your waste
